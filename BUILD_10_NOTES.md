## App Review Notes (private — Apple reviewer only)

PROPERTYDNA v1.0.2 (build 25) — WEB-PARITY UPDATE, STILL FULLY FREE

This update adds informational pages (Owner Portal, Data Integrity methodology, Network) and a market heat-map view toggle (Score / price-per-sqft / sales velocity) to match the website. It does NOT change the purchasing model:

- The app remains 100% free. There are NO in-app purchases, NO subscriptions, and NO paid or externally-purchased content unlocked anywhere in the iOS app.
- Every screen — heat maps, Owner Portal, Data Integrity, Network, reports — is fully visible to every user at no cost. The client forces free-tier state on iOS regardless of any web subscription tied to the same email, so there is no gated/blurred premium content and no prompt to purchase anything.
- Sign-in (Apple, Google, or 6-digit email code) only saves the user's free report; it never unlocks paid content.
- Sign in with Apple is offered wherever Google sign-in is offered (Guideline 4.0).

HOW TO TEST
1. Sign in via "or use email," enter a reviewer email, retrieve the 6-digit code, and enter it. (Apple and Google sign-in also work.)
2. Search any U.S. address (e.g., "100 W Vista Chino, Palm Springs, CA") to view a complete property report — every section visible, free, no upsell.
3. Open the market heat map and toggle Score / $-per-sqft / velocity — all views are free.

No paid or external content is required to evaluate the app.
