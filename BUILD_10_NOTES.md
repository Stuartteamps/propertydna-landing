## App Review Notes (private — Apple reviewer only)

PROPERTYDNA v1.0.1 (build 23) — ADDS EMAIL SIGN-IN OPTION

This update adds one thing: an email sign-in option alongside the existing Sign in with Apple and Sign in with Google. On the sign-in screen, tap "or use email," enter any email address, and we email a 6-digit one-time code. Enter that code in the app to create a free account.

This does NOT change the app's purchasing model:
- The app remains free. There are no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no paid content in the iOS app.
- Sign-in is used only to save the user's free report and personalize the experience — it does NOT unlock any externally-purchased content. Every user, signed in or not, sees the same free experience (the client forces free-tier state on iOS regardless of any web subscription on that email).
- Sign in with Apple is offered wherever Google sign-in is offered, satisfying Guideline 4.0.

HOW TO TEST
1. On the sign-in screen, tap "or use email," enter a reviewer email, retrieve the 6-digit code from that inbox, and enter it. (Sign in with Apple and Google also work.)
2. Search any U.S. address (e.g., "100 W Vista Chino, Palm Springs, CA") to view a complete property report — every section visible, free, no upsell.

No paid or external content is required to evaluate the app.
