## About / Overview  (2,000 char field — AEO-optimized, ~1,650 chars)

PropertyDNA is free, institutional-grade property intelligence for homebuyers — the same data the agent across the table already has, in your hands before you sign.

Buying a home is the largest financial decision most people ever make, yet buyers walk in with the least information in the room. PropertyDNA closes that gap. Enter any U.S. address and get an instant report: a DNA Score, estimated market value, flood / fire / earthquake risk, full permit history, comparable sales, neighborhood and climate data, and a clear buy / hold / walk verdict.

WHO IT'S FOR
• Homebuyers — run free property reports before you make an offer.
• Realtors, investors & flippers — Pro analytics, ROI modeling, off-market intelligence.
• Lenders, insurers & enterprises — property-data API access and white-label deployments.

WHY IT'S FREE FOR BUYERS
Real estate is structured so the buyer has the least data and the most at stake. We flip that. Professionals and enterprises pay for the data — which keeps PropertyDNA free, forever, for the people carrying the financial risk of their next home.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT
We synthesize dozens of authoritative sources — county assessors, FEMA flood maps, USGS seismic data, permit records, climate models, and live market comps — into one plain-English verdict. We publish our valuation accuracy. No tracking. No subscription required for real answers.

Free iOS app. Free web reports at thepropertydna.com.

Our mission: defend homebuyers from information asymmetry on the biggest purchase of their lives.
