## The three checks every Florida buyer needs to make (4:00 – 6:00)

**DAN:**
> "If you're buying anywhere in Florida in 2026, you need three answers before you sign a contract.
>
> Number one: What carrier tier is the zip code? If it's Citizens-only or carrier-tier 3, your insurance cost is going to be twice what your agent told you to budget. Get a quote from an actual carrier before you sign, not after.
>
> Number two: Has the FEMA flood zone been revised since Helene and Milton? If you're in a new AE designation, your lender will require flood insurance, your premium will be $2,000 to $8,000, and the property's resale value is going to reflect that within 12 months.
>
> Number three: Who is the carrier the seller is on, and are they accepting new policies? If the seller is on a carrier that's non-renewing the area, the policy doesn't transfer to you. You start from zero."
