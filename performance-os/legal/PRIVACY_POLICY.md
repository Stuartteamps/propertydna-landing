## What we collect
- **Account:** your email address and an authentication token.
- **Health & fitness data you connect or enter:** sleep, heart-rate variability, heart rate,
  resting heart rate, workouts, steps, activity/energy, VO₂ max, respiratory rate, body metrics,
  and derived readiness scores.
- **Content you create:** meal photos and their nutrition estimates, workouts, journal entries,
  recovery sessions (sauna/cold plunge), and lab results you choose to enter.
- **Sensitive data you choose to enter** (e.g., medications) is stored separately and treated as
  confidential. We do not use it for any purpose other than showing it back to you.

We do **not** collect precise location, contacts, or advertising identifiers.

## How Apple Health data is used
Health data imported from Apple Health is used **only** to compute your readiness score, nutrition
targets, and recommendations inside the app. Consistent with Apple's requirements, Health data is
**never** used for advertising or marketing, and is **never** sold or shared with third parties or
data brokers.

## How we use data
- To calculate readiness, nutrition targets, training summaries, and recommendations.
- To operate your account and sync your data across your devices.
- We do **not** sell your data, use it for advertising, or track you across other apps or websites.

## AI processing
Meal photos and text you submit for nutrition estimation, and optional voice notes for
transcription, may be processed by an AI provider to generate estimates. In the default
configuration these run on a mock provider and no data leaves your server. If a real AI provider is
enabled by the operator, the relevant content is sent to that provider solely to produce your
result. Estimates are approximate and clearly labeled as such.

## Storage and security
Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and stored on our backend
(`<hosting provider/region>`). Access is scoped to your account. We keep audit logs of sensitive
actions (login, export, deletion) and do not write private health values to application logs.

## Your controls
- **Export:** download all your data in-app (Profile → Privacy) or via the account export endpoint.
- **Delete:** delete individual meal images, or delete your entire account and its data, in-app.
- **Revoke integrations:** disconnect Apple Health or Google Calendar at any time.

## Data retention
We retain your data until you delete it or delete your account, after which it is removed from our
active systems.

## Children
Arete is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction).

## Medical disclaimer
Arete is for education, wellness, and fitness tracking only. It does not diagnose, treat, or
prescribe, and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Consult a licensed clinician
for medical decisions.
