# Arete — Support

Need help with Arete? We're happy to assist.

- **Email:** `<support@your-domain>`
- **Response time:** typically within 2–3 business days.

## Common questions

**How is my readiness score calculated?**
From your sleep, heart-rate variability, resting heart rate, and recent training load, each
compared to your personal baseline. If there isn't enough data yet, Arete shows "not enough data"
rather than a misleading number. It's a wellness signal, not a medical measurement.

**Are the nutrition numbers exact?**
No. Photo- and text-based nutrition values are estimates and are always editable before you save.

**How do I connect Apple Health?**
Profile → Connect & sync Apple Health, then approve the permissions. You can disconnect anytime.

**How do I export or delete my data?**
Profile → Privacy → Export my data, or delete your account (removes all your data).

**Is Arete medical advice?**
No. Arete is for education, wellness, and fitness tracking only and is not a substitute for
professional medical care. Consult a licensed clinician for medical decisions.

## Report a problem
Email `<support@your-domain>` with your device model, iOS version, and a short description (and a
screenshot if relevant).
