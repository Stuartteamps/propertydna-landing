## Realistic outcomes (be honest with yourself)

- Cold DM response rate to A-tier (1M+): 5-10%
- Cold DM response rate to micro (50-500K): 20-40%
- Conversion to actual mention: 30-50% of responders
- Installs per mention: 100-2,000 depending on creator engagement

**Target**: 50 cold DMs → 15-20 responses → 5-8 actual mentions → 1,000-5,000 installs in launch week.
