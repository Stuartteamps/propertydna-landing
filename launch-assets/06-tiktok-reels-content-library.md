## Production notes

- **Shoot vertical 9:16.** Native to TikTok, Reels, Shorts. Repurpose to all three.
- **First 3 seconds are everything.** No "Hey guys, today I want to talk about..." — start with the pattern interrupt.
- **No background music for talking-head shots.** Authority comes from quiet.
- **Captions burned in.** 85% of social video is watched muted.
- **One CTA per video.** Always the App Store link via bio.
- **Filming order.** Shoot 5 videos per session for batching. Videos 1, 2, 9 are highest-priority — record those first.
- **Posting cadence.** 1 video/day for 14 days. Maximum algorithm signal.
