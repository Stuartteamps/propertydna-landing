## LinkedIn

### Long form
Today PropertyDNA goes live on the Apple App Store. Free for every American homebuyer.

For sixty years residential real estate has been defined by a single structural imbalance: the agent on the other side of the transaction has the data, and the buyer does not. They know the comparable sales velocity. The permit history. The absorption-rate trajectory. The seller's motivation. The buyer gets a glossy PDF and a smile.

That imbalance has cost American buyers an estimated $200 billion in over-payments and undisclosed risk exposure. We cannot recover the past sixty years. We can put the data in the buyer's pocket starting today.

PropertyDNA delivers a complete property intelligence report — valuation with confidence labels, FEMA flood zones, CalFire wildfire severity, USGS seismic exposure, county permit history, comparable trajectory, school ratings, and a confidence-scored five-year value trajectory — for any property in our 1.67-million-parcel index.

Every metric is traceable to a named public source: RentCast MLS, US Census ACS, FEMA NFHL, CalFire FHSZ, USGS seismic models, county Assessor CREST APIs, the National Weather Service. No black-box scoring.

The iOS app is 100% free with every feature unlocked. No in-app purchases, no subscriptions, no upsells, no advertising. Real estate professionals pay for power tools on the web — that funds the consumer mission.

Download free on iOS: https://apps.apple.com/app/id6768064079
