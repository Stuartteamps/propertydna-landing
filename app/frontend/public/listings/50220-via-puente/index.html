Pricing Position

La Quinta's broader market sells in roughly 73–84 days at about 2% under list. Palmilla floor plans run ~2,900–4,600 sf; this sits near the top of that range on one of the community's scarce true-lakefront lots. At $2.5M / $553.96 per sf the home is positioned at the top of Palmilla — reflecting the lakefront premium, the two-primary + casita layout, the upgrades, and the barely-used condition. With 109 days on market at the original price, there is genuine negotiating room for a serious buyer.

A Nationally Recognized Community

When it was developed (2003–2005), Palmilla earned the “Best in American Living” award from the National Association of Home Builders and Professional Builder, was recognized as one of America's best single-family detached communities, and won a 2005 ASLA Honor Award for its landscape design. Built around a 7-acre lake across 72 landscaped acres, it remains one of La Quinta's most distinctive addresses.
